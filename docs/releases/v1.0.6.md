# Bonjou v1.0.6 Release Notes

## Highlights

- `@setname` lets you change your LAN handle mid-session; Bonjou persists the value, refreshes transfer metadata, and immediately re-announces it to peers.
- Discovery and transfer services now guard local identity updates, eliminating rare race conditions and ensuring outgoing envelopes always reflect the latest username and IP.
- Progress output is streamlined to a single live line with graceful fallbacks when readline is unavailable, keeping the prompt tidy during long transfers.
- A comprehensive installation guide (`docs/install-guide.md`) covers Homebrew, Scoop, Debian packages, manual artifacts, and offline distribution steps.

## Upgrade Instructions

- **Homebrew**
  ```bash
  brew update
  brew upgrade bonjou
  ```
- **Scoop**
  ```powershell
  scoop update
  scoop update bonjou
  ```
- **Manual**
  Download the appropriate artifact from the v1.0.6 release, place it on your PATH, and relaunch `bonjou`.

Encourage every teammate to refresh so usernames remain in sync and transfer output stays tidy across the LAN.
